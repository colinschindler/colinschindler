American Dreams
 Talking about hopes, dreams, and aspirations in the land of opportunity.

" Okay, well, smoker jumpers are part of a crew, what we like to call initial attack. And what their main role is, whenever there is a smoke report or someone calls in a fire and it is out in the wilderness where there are no roads. Where they don’t allow gas powered machines. They’ll send in the smoke jumpers for quick and early responses so that the fires don’t get to a point where they are unmanageable, like they usually do get. That is their main role and duty. There is a lot of waiting around but it’s usually paid off by getting to jump out of an airplane into a fire. "
