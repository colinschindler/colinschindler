Caravanning Sedona 2017-2-14

We spent about a week camping out near Sedona with varying groups of people. Lots of hiking about; campfires with music, smores, and weeners (primarily vegetarian); and Sky King buzzing around with his drone ensued.

After Sedona we headed west and checked out the towns of Cottonwood and Jerome. Jerome reminded me a lot of Deadwood back home in the Black Hills of South Dakota. It is a small little mining town built in the side of a mountain full of saloons, art, and trinkets. We also stopped and checked out Prescott and decided to make a stop on the way out to further explore.
