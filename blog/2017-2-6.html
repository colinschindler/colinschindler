I hadn't seen a cactus before we started this trip, I mean a real big cactus, and now we are doing trail runs and dogding dangerous plants almost daily. This place is cool, but it doesn't sound like we'd want to be here in the summer.

Desert Sunset 5/5

We have done lots of desert hikes, but we pushed it past sunset on this one and the turn out was beyond words. The trail was lit by the cresent moon plenty well for us to see our steps and the coyotes began yipping and howling in the distance. It was another world and the only danger was looking up at the stars too long and having a misstep. We crossed a man made bridge and stopped to take some pictures.
