First warm day in Colorado. 2016-12-9

The heater has been doing the job. I talked to Ray @ Espar of Michigan and he said it was most likely the high altitude that is giving us trouble. There is a high elevation sensor you can wire in that detects elevation and adjusts the fuel pump to give you the optimal fuel/air mixture. Unfortunately, it is $300, which is too expensive.

On the bright side, it was actually warm enough to be outside today. So I went on a bike ride.
