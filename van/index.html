What we're doing

We built the van to be self-sufficient while covering all our basic needs. The Sprinter is a 25 mpg diesel vehicle with a range of 400-500 miles. Stealth camping is simple and hassle free. Michelle books gigs along our route and has been promoting her first cd entitled: "Wake Up". When windows of opportunity allow we take the van into nature and stage it as a base camp for my photography and for hiking and exploring. Lastly, we have our touring bicycles and hope to do micro tours of specific areas.
