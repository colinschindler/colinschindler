Print Types

Fuji Crystal Archive Professional Super Type PD Luster Vivid - color reproduction, brilliant whites, with a semi-gloss luster finish; this paper offers a sharper, crisper image.

Canvas Gallery Wraps - canvas has superior color rendition, rich blacks and a smooth gloss finish. Our lab prints on high quality white poly-cotton canvas to ensure that the saturation and sharpness is positively awe-inspiring. All of our canvas is printed and stretched right here in the lab for impeccable quality assurance.

Our canvas manufacturer partners with American Forests to help rebuild forests. They do this by planting a tree for every roll of canvas material that our print lab uses.

Print Quality & Process

I work with a print lab that "uses industry leading photographic papers and top of the line printing technology." Most sizes between 5×7 and 30×40 can be mounted and all orders are shipped flat unless they contain a print that is larger than 24″x36″. All photo work is completed and soft-proofed on a Retina display which is calibrated every 2 weeks to ensure color accuracy.
