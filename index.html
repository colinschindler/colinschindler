  • Great Sand Dunes in Winter

    Colorado - San Luis Valley

    The Great Sand Dunes of southern Colorado are the tallest sand dunes in North America rising 750 feet off the San Luis Valley floor. In December, Christmas day actually, they were primarily covered with snow except for the higher peaks. The sunset illuminated the Sangre de Cristo Mountains behind the dunes with Mt. Herard to the left and Mt. Zwischen to the right.
